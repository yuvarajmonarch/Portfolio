HungerLess

Hungrless is a public welfare project focused on redistributing unused food from venues, such as marriage halls, to support old age homes and public pet adoption centers. By connecting donors with facilities that need resources, the project aims to minimize food waste and provide sustenance for both people and animals in need. Youth volunteers actively participate, and fundraising efforts help sustain its operations as an NGO, designed using Figma.

Users can view, like, and share videos, with search and recommendation functionalities implemented for a smooth experience. AI Powered Soldiers Health Monitoring System

AI-Powered Soldiers Health Monitoring System is an IoT-based project designed to enhance soldier safety. Utilizing wearable sensors, it continuously monitors vital signs and employs AI algorithms to analyze health data in real-time, providing actionable insights and alerts for timely medical attention in high-stress environments, improving overall operational effectiveness.
